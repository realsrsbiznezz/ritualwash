How to use

The ritual.

1

Measure

Use the cap to measure — 1.5 oz to the fill line for a standard load. For large or heavily soiled loads, fill to the top.

2

Add to drum or dispenser

Pour directly into the drum before adding clothes, or into the detergent compartment. Works with all HE and standard machines.

3

Wash as normal

Any temperature, any cycle. The scent is designed to hold through the wash and linger on fabric for days — not just the first wear.

4

The ritual part

Open the washer. Notice the scent. That's the part nobody tells you laundry can feel like.
